About Doktor Budapest

Doktor Budapest was founded in 2016 and is one of Sweden’s biggest providers of digital healthcare and also have physical healthcare clinics across Sweden as well as digital operations across the world.

Our ambition

Our ambition is to improve healthcare worldwide, and to make it more efficient. By making the first contact digital, we save time and resources for both the individual and our society at large.

More patients receive care faster, while traditional healthcare is relieved. This frees up resources for patients who really need physical appointments.

I'm passionate about creating a better healthcare system

Martin Lindman, CEO
